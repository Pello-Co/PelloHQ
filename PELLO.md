# Pello — Company & Product Overview

## Brand
- **Name:** Pello
- **Meaning:** Latin — "to propel / drive / expel" — referencing the growth Pello provides to businesses
- **Main domain:** pello.co

## What Pello.co Is
The parent brand that brings together the full product suite. Users enter their website URL → AI scrapes and researches their business → Pello shows tailored demos of each product in the suite.

Example: if the URL is a travel company, the PelloSEO demo would show "Best Activities in {London}".

Pello.co is designed to advertise how all products work in a streamlined, personalised way.

---

## Product Suite

### PelloSEO (pelloseo.com)
Programmatic SEO platform.
- Learns about the client's platform
- Generates 10s–100s of SEO and AI-optimised Page Templates
- Each template produces hundreds of pages (e.g. "Best Activities in {Location}" → 100s of location pages)
- Available in multiple languages
- Goal: quickly and automatically scale a brand's SEO to tens of thousands of pages across countries
- Very low effort for the client — they just approve the content type

### PelloReach (pelloreach.com)
Email outreach engine.
- No custom tech at the start
- Uses ZapMail to purchase hundreds of domains/mailboxes and warm them up
- Imports into a tool like ReachInbox
- Finds leads and emails customers at scale

### PelloPublish (pellopublish.com)
Automated social content generation.
- Learns about the business, writing style, tone
- Starts with blog posts / articles, grows to all content types: images, reels, videos, shorts, carousels, UGC, product placement
- Both manual and automated methods available

### PelloSocial
Server farm-based social media growth.
- Server farms with thousands of mobile phones
- Creates accounts for the client
- Creates fake UGC personas with AI
- AI automates phone usage
- Places IPs in target markets
- Posts relevant content for the client

### PelloPitch (pellopitch.co)
AI bespoke landing pages and onboarding flows.
- Similar to the AI onboarding flow of pello.co
- Built for client businesses

### PelloAds
Mass-scale ad testing and management.
- Tests thousands of different ad types
- Manages ad campaigns
- Improves ROI and conversion

### PelloFlow
Custom automation and agent builds.
- Builds any type of automation for a client
- Sets up business automation end-to-end
- Custom agents bespoke to each client
- Custom tools per client

### PelloBuild
Custom application development.
- Builds any application for a client
- Engineering team

## Technical Architecture
- All products are hosted on separate domains
- All products share a single authentication system — one account, logged in across the entire Pello suite
