# Pello — Project Ideas

Ideas for expanding the Pello product suite and ecosystem.

---

## New Products

### PelloRank
AI-powered review and reputation management.
- Monitors reviews across Google, Trustpilot, G2, App Store, etc.
- Auto-generates personalised response drafts
- Flags negative sentiment for urgent handling
- Tracks reputation score over time

### PelloLead
AI lead scoring and enrichment platform.
- Enriches inbound leads with company/contact data
- Scores leads by fit and intent
- Routes leads to the right sales rep or flow automatically
- Integrates with CRMs (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive)

### PelloChat
AI-powered customer support and sales chat widget.
- Trained on the client's website, docs, and product knowledge
- Handles support tickets autonomously
- Escalates to human agents when needed
- Tracks conversation analytics and conversion

### PelloPrice
Dynamic pricing and competitive intelligence tool.
- Monitors competitor pricing in real time
- Recommends optimal pricing based on demand and competition
- A/B tests price points
- Particularly useful for e-commerce and SaaS

### PelloRetain
Customer churn prediction and retention automation.
- Analyses product usage and engagement signals
- Predicts at-risk customers before they churn
- Triggers automated retention campaigns (email, offers, check-ins)
- Integrates with PelloReach and PelloPublish

### PelloInsight
Unified business intelligence dashboard.
- Aggregates data from all Pello products in one place
- AI-generated weekly performance summaries
- Competitor benchmarking
- Goal tracking and forecasting

### PelloAffiliate
Affiliate and referral programme management.
- Creates and manages affiliate programmes for clients
- Tracks referrals, clicks, and conversions
- Automates commission payouts
- Provides affiliate portal for partners

### PelloVoice
AI voice agents for sales and support.
- Outbound cold calling at scale
- Inbound call handling and triage
- Appointment booking and follow-up
- Integrates with PelloReach for multi-channel outreach

### PelloTrust
Social proof and conversion optimisation.
- Collects and displays testimonials, case studies, and reviews
- Auto-generates social proof widgets for client websites
- A/B tests proof formats for conversion lift
- Works with PelloPitch landing pages

### PelloHire
AI-assisted recruitment automation.
- Writes and posts job descriptions
- Screens and scores CVs
- Conducts first-round AI interviews
- Shortlists candidates for human review

## Platform Expansions

### Pello Marketplace
- A marketplace where clients can browse, mix, and match Pello products
- Partner integrations (agencies, tools, data providers)
- White-label options for agencies to resell Pello products

### Pello Partner Network
- Certified agency and consultant partners
- Revenue share for referring clients to Pello
- Co-branded case studies and go-to-market support

### Pello API & Developer Platform
- Public API access to all Pello products
- Webhooks and event streams
- Developer docs and SDK
- Enables third-party integrations and custom builds on top of Pello

### Pello Mobile App
- Unified mobile dashboard for all Pello products
- Push notifications for key events (lead alerts, content approvals, rank changes)
- On-the-go content approvals for PelloPublish and PelloSEO

## AI & Infrastructure

### Pello AI Brain (Internal)
- Shared AI layer across all products
- Client business profile built once, used everywhere
- The more products a client uses, the smarter Pello gets about their business
- Enables cross-product personalisation and recommendations

### Pello Data Network
- Anonymised, aggregated benchmarking data across all clients
- Industry-level insights sold as a separate data product
- Powers smarter defaults and recommendations across the suite
